Calipers acceptance checks

Select each value, right-click, compare against the expectation.

Lengths

  1. 8mm: root reads "Calipers: 8 mm"; children include 0.314961 in and 314.961 thou; km and mi live under "More units".
  2. 3 inch: the "Ø circumference" submenu title carries 9.42478 in.
  3. 1 1/2": parsed as 1.5 in.
  4. 42: root reads "(assumed)"; an "Assume unit" submenu appears at the bottom.
  5. hello: single disabled item, no submenu.
  6. 1,000 mm: thousands separator handled; 1 m among children.
  7. 8 minutes: must NOT parse as 8 metres (disabled item).

Electronics

  1. 100nF: children include 0.1 µF and 100000 pF; F under "More units"; no circle geometry.
  2. 4k7: parsed as 4.7 kΩ; children include 4700 Ω.
  3. 10 kohm: same as 10 kΩ.
  4. 2.2mH, 16 MHz, 3.3v, 20mA, 250mW: each parsed in its own dimension.

Assumed units

  1. Select 42, open "Assume unit", pick Capacitance then µF. A toast confirms. Right-click 42 again: root now reads "Calipers: 42 µF (assumed)".
  2. Switch it back to Length, mm, the same way.

General

  1. Click any leaf: clipboard holds exactly the label text, this selection survives, a toast appears bottom-right.
  2. Right-click twice in rapid succession: no stale or duplicated menu.
